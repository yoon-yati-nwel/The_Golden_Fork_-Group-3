Fried Chicken

    Fried chicken is a popular dish made by coating pieces of chicken in flour or batter and then cooking them 
in hot oil until they’re crispy and golden. People enjoy it in many ways—some like it spicy, some prefer it mild, 
and others love it extra crunchy. It can be made with drumsticks, wings, thighs, or even boneless pieces. Fried 
chicken is often served with sides like coleslaw, mashed potatoes, biscuits, or corn on the cob. Some people dip 
it in sauces like honey mustard, barbecue, or hot sauce to add even more flavor. While it’s a comfort food favorite, 
fried chicken also provides protein and iron, though it can be high in fat depending on how it’s cooked. Different 
styles of fried chicken exist around the world. Southern-style fried chicken is famous in the United States, while 
Korean fried chicken is known for its double-fried crispiness and sweet-spicy glaze. No matter the style, fried 
chicken is loved for its satisfying crunch and juicy taste.
 
Fun Facts about Fried Chicken

    There are also some fun facts about fried chicken! The word “fried” comes from the Old English word frīgan, 
meaning to cook in fat or oil. Fried chicken became popular in the U.S. in the 1800s, especially in the South, 
where it was often served at family gatherings and celebrations. The most famous fried chicken brand, KFC, was started by
Colonel Sanders in 1930, and his secret recipe of 11 herbs and spices is still a mystery today.In Japan, fried 
chicken is a popular Christmas meal, and people even pre-order buckets from KFC weeks in advance. The world’s 
largest serving of fried chicken weighed over 100 pounds! Some countries have their own versions, like karaage 
in Japan or ayam goreng in Indonesia. There’s even a National Fried Chicken Day celebrated on July 6th in the U.S., 
showing just how much people love this crispy dish.                                                                                                                                                                          

Whether it’s homemade or from a restaurant, fried chicken is a meal that brings joy and flavor to tables all over 
the world.
